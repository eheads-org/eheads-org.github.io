About eHeads

Empowering Tech Leaders to Thrive

eHeads is a fellowship designed to support engineering leaders in building high-performing teams, navigating team challenges, and transitioning into tech leadership roles. Through peer-to-peer learning, mentorship, and shared experiences, eHeads members gain the skills and confidence to excel in their roles.
